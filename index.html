Smart Recycling Bin

New Solution to Help Everyone Recycle Better And Protect Our Environment

What is Smart Recycling Bin?

There are around 4 billion tons of waste are produced in the world every year and poor waste management leads to the contamination of water and increases greenhouse gases in the atmosphere.
Smart Recycling Bin is a new app that are helpful in improving waste management and encourage individuals to learn the correct waste sorting.

Smart Recycling Bin Features:

  • Use image-based sensors to identify the correct bins to increase accuracy of waste-sorting
  • Prompting accurate waste sorting behavior encourage users learn what items can/cannot be recycled
  • Less waste goes into landfills and reduce green house gas emissions
  • Prompting accurate waste sorting behavior improves air and water quality

Impact of Poor Waste Management:

Around 4 Billion tons of waste are produced every year

Inadequate waste management systems can result in a higher volume of waste being disposed of improperly, exacerbating the issue.

1 in 4 items are thrown in a recycling bin isn't recyclable

Many people may not be fully informed about what can and cannot be recycled. Confusing recycling instructions on bins and limited sorting knowledge increase risk of improper waste disposal

Poor waste management pollutes water and soil

Improper disposal of waste, such as dumping untreated sewage or industrial waste into water bodies, can contaminate surface water and groundwater.
